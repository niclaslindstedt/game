type: Changed
title: Level-up light explosion
---

Leveling up now detonates a blinding explosion of light: the horde is hurled back on a shockwave (knocked back, never harmed), the screen floods with a holy-gold flash, god-rays, and a pillar of light rising off the hero, and the LEVEL UP! chooser rises out of the fading glare — all under a grander triumphant fanfare and a heavier celebratory haptic.
