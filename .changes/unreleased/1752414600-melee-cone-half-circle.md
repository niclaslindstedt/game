---
type: Changed
title: Gentler melee AoE cone, capped at a half circle
---

INTELLIGENCE now widens a melee weapon's AoE cone gently and saturates it at a half circle (a 180° sweep) instead of a full one, so reaching that cap takes a deep-endgame INT investment and a swing always leaves a back arc uncovered; the developer WEAPON SWING animation's slash now renders on the blade itself and widens with the cone.
