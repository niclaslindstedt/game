---
type: Added
title: Always-on magic talents
---

The INTELLIGENCE tree gains its first always-on conjurations: ORBITING FLAMES rings the hero with fire that burns what it touches, and STORM CALL hurls lightning at the nearest foe on its own — both hands-free, scaling with rank and INTELLIGENCE, and stacking on top of any legendary that already grants the same power. A deep-INT hero can now stand in the horde and let the spells do the killing.
