type: Changed
title: Sharper Mars sprites
---

Redrew the four weakest-reading Mars sprites: the MINING ROVER now looms as a tracked drill rig instead of reading like fodder, the SCOUT ROVER reads as a clean camera-on-wheels, the FEMBOT is an uncanny kiss-blowing android in a nightgown, and the TERRARIUM shrine is a carved lizard-god idol with its gold tithe.
