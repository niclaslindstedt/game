---
type: Changed
title: Artifacts settle the level-99 class order
---

The artifact melee affinity is now a per-class rate (melee > ranged > magic), so a hero who picks up even a couple of common, easy-to-find relics settles the endgame pecking order into melee ahead of ranged ahead of magic — the casters that peak mid/late yield the top of level 99 back to the physical lanes, well before the rare apex relics.
