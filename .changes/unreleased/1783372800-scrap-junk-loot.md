---
type: Added
title: One-tap SCRAP button in the bag
---

The inventory's BAG header gains a SCRAP button that clears every piece the hero has outgrown in one tap — loot that is worse than what's worn in its slot — while sparing keepers: upgrades, side-grades, empty-slot fills, passive trinkets, and unique/legendary trophies. The button shows how many pieces would be culled and stays disabled when the bag is already clean.
