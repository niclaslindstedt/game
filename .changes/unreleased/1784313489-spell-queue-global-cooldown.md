---
type: Changed
title: Spells cast once per press, with a queue and global cooldown
breaking: false
---

Casting a spell now fires it exactly ONCE per press instead of leaving it "on" until mana ran out; pressing several spells queues them to fire in order (draining while mana lasts, then waiting for regen), and every cast shares a global cooldown so you cannot chain-fire two spells instantly.
