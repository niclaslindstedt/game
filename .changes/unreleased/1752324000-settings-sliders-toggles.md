---
type: Changed
title: Settings sliders and ON/OFF switches
---

The SOUND music and SFX volumes are now drag sliders (reusing the BALANCE track) instead of quarter-step cycles, and every straight ON/OFF setting (DEBUG MODE, AUTO LEVEL STATS, CHARACTER WEAPON, WEAPON SWING, VIBRATION, XP ON KILL) now shows an iOS-style pixel toggle switch you can flip by clicking or steering with ←/→.
