---
type: Changed
title: The horde eases off at bosses on EASY and MEDIUM
---

On the two gentlest rungs the swarm now **loses its legs the moment you engage an elite or boss** — drop to **10% chase speed on EASY, 50% on MEDIUM** — so you can push past the pile-on and just run to the set piece instead of being dog-piled at it. The mercy only kicks in once the encounter has actually started (the elite/boss is awake, wounded, or you've stepped inside its aggro range), so an idle player away from a set piece is still overrun as before. HARD and up give no ground.
