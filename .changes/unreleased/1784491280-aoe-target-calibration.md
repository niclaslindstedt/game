---
type: Changed
title: "AoE target calibration"
---

Added a wind-tunnel that measures how many foes a melee swing actually hits as a function of its cone angle — arming the real autopilot with probe weapons of varying `sweepDeg` and recording the true in-cone count on every swing — so the damage-budget model's AoE assumption is grounded in measured play instead of a hand-guess.
