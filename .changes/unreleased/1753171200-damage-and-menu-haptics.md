type: Added
title: Buzz on damage and menu presses; a subtler dialogue tick
---

The native app now buzzes when the hero takes a hit — scaled to the share of the health bar the blow cost, from a faint flick for a graze to a heavy two-beat jolt for a near-fatal hit — and taps under each title-menu press. The dialogue typewriter crawl now ticks with the Taptic Engine's gentle selection cue, so a line reads as a soft chatter rather than a row of hard knocks.
