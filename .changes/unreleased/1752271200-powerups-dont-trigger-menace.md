---
type: Changed
title: Powerups no longer trigger menace
---

The screen-nuke bomb and the damage powerups (fire orbs, storm cell) no longer heat the escalation meter: their damage and kills are still booked for the run stats but held out of the menace rolling DPS/kill-rate, the overkill jolt, the dinner-bell lure, and the evolution ratchet. Clearing the screen with a consumable no longer escalates the horde the player never out-fought by hand — menace still answers only the hero's own weapon.
