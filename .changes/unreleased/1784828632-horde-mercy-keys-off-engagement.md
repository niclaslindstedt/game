---
type: Changed
title: The gentle-rung horde mercy keys off engaging a boss, not standing near one
---

On EASY and MEDIUM the swarm now eases off only once you actually **trade blows** with an elite or boss — you wound it or it lands a hit on you — instead of the moment you step inside its aggro radius. Mere proximity no longer slows the horde, so you can't farm the area next to a set piece at crawl speed without committing to the fight.
