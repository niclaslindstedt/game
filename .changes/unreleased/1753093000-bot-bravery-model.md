type: Changed
title: Autopilot's stamina caution scales with how well the run is going
---

The autopilot's stamina reserve floor now slides with a bravery read — how hard its blows hit the local health bars, how fast it has been shredding them over the last minute, and the medkits, stamina potions, and powerups in its pockets: a naked rookie paces cautiously at ~35%, a kitted-out shredder digs to ~10% and engages spotted packs at ~70% instead of demanding a full pool.
