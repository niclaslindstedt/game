---
type: Added
title: In-HUD weapon switcher
---

The HUD weapon icon is now a prominent bordered slot tinted by class (yellow melee, red ranged, purple magic); tap it to expand a switcher of your other carried weapons — ordered by damage, each showing its stat-scaled damage — and tap one to equip it on the spot. Weapon info in the inventory now shows the damage a weapon deals in your hands (stats and affixes folded in) with a `+x` bonus hint, matching how combat and auto-equip already rank weapons.
