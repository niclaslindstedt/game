type: Added
title: Artifacts make melee the endgame king
---

Every worn artifact-tier relic now multiplies a melee weapon's damage, so a bruiser who commits to the level-99 artifact chase reclaims the top of the endgame from the casters — a full set of relics puts melee clearly on top, while a mage in artifact armour gets nothing (it rewards actually swinging the legend).
