---
type: Fixed
title: Auto-equip no longer swaps to weak spread weapons
---

Auto-equip credited a ranged spread weapon (a shotgun's pellets, pierce, or chain) its full theoretical target count, so a pump shotgun with a quarter of your weapon's per-target damage could displace it on a paper tie. Spread weapons are now scored on the fraction of that potential they realistically land, so auto-equip keeps a reliable single-target weapon unless the spread gun genuinely out-budgets it.
