title: Quality-graded pickup badge
---

The pickup card now dresses a find by BOTH of its axes: within the plain regular tier the make quality drives the look — a broken piece gets a dull, cracked, dashed frame, a crude one stays dim, superior glows and perfect really shines — while magic/rare/unique escalate the halo, sparkles, and flames, and a legendary is revealed in a shockwave explosion with every effect turned to eleven.
