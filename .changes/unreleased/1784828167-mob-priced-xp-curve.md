type: Changed
title: Predictable leveling pace
---

Every XP faucet is now mob-priced — elites pay ×5 and bosses ×10 their own mob-level XP and golden arrows a flat 5 mob-kills' worth (all authored in leveling.yaml) instead of shares of the level bar — and the level curve is retuned so kills-per-level starts at ~20 and compounds ~4.5% per level, keeping the endgame wall past 70.
