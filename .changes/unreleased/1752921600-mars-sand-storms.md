title: Mars sand storms
---

Small, animated sand storms now roll across MARS. A churning dust gust drifts in from off-screen every so often and sweeps the hero's surroundings — walk clear and it's harmless, but stand in its path and it flattens you: a bite of damage and a KNOCKOUT that leaves you lying prone on the dirt for two seconds, unable to move, attack, or cast, while the storm passes over you and fades away.
