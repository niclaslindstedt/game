---
type: Fixed
title: Purposeful autopilot jumps
---

The autoplay bot no longer wastes stamina on jumps that go nowhere: every jump is now committed to a purpose before takeoff — fleeing a pack down an open lane or (ranged/magic only, since a melee blade can't swing mid-air) repositioning over a contact — steered at its committed landing ground for the whole flight, and refused entirely when a wall blocks the lane so it would only rise in place.
