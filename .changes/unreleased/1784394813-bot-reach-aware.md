type: Fixed
title: Autopilot fights at weapon range instead of diving into the pack
---

The autoplay bot no longer charges into the middle of the horde. During a scripted disarmed opening it holds at the pack's edge and lets the vanguard come to it (rather than burying itself unarmed), and once armed a ranged loadout holds near its weapon reach and thins the crowd from a distance while pushing the objective, instead of hugging the front line. New positioning knobs live in `bot.yaml` (per-level overridable).
