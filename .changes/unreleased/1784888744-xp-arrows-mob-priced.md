---
type: Changed
title: Golden arrows are priced to the mob that dropped them
---

Golden XP arrows now pay a few kills' worth of the mob that shed them rather than always the hero's own level, so an arrow dropped by an outgrown map's low-level horde is worth only that mob's little (a grey mob's arrow trickles to nothing) instead of a full at-level ding — grinding old ground can no longer over-level you.
