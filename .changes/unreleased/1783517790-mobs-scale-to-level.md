---
type: Changed
title: The horde scales to your level
---

Regular enemies now toughen as you level up — more health (and, since kill XP tracks health, more XP), and richer loot to match — so leftover levels no longer turn the swarm into a walkover. This is a steady progression floor on top of the existing menace system, which still answers a moment-to-moment rampage with an evolved, lured crowd.
