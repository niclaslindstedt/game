Casting joins the game: INTELLIGENCE now sizes a MANA pool and unlocks a spell every 10 points (25 in all — single-target, area, and defensive), cast from a HUD spell bar (tap to cast, long-press to reassign a slot) and echoed by a "SPELL UNLOCKED" reveal each milestone. A new seventh stat, SPIRIT, regenerates mana (after a lull in casting) and health, and rolls on caster gear. A blue-gatorade MANA POTION drops and refills the pool, docked beside the medkit. Every spell ships a themed pixel icon and an element-tinted cast effect. (Saved runs from before this release cannot be resumed — the hero grew a mana pool, spirit, and a spell bar.)
