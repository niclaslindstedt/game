Loot is now Diablo-shaped: every level introduces five themed base weapons with level requirements (earthly arms at SpaceZ HQ, 70s hardware on the moon, AI-forged weapons on Mars, historic and fantasy arms in the rift — including shotgun spreads, piercing rails, homing darts, and chain lightning), item tiers unlock by monster level (magic 5, rare 10, unique 15, legendary 25), drops carry an item level that sizes their magic bonuses, and bosses pledge per-tier drops that can exceed 100%.
