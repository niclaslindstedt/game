New achievement shelves: WARDROBE badges for equipping each gear slot the first time, filling every slot at once, and wearing full outfits of all-magic, all-rare, and all-unique quality — plus COMBAT damage ladders for the hardest single hit (50 to 5,000), the biggest one-strike burst from nukes, sweeps, and volleys (100 to 25,000), and lifetime damage dealt (10K to 50M), with thresholds grounded in the game's real damage model.
