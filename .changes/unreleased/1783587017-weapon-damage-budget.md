---
type: Changed
title: Weapon damage rebalanced to a level budget
breaking: false
---

Every weapon's damage now follows an effective-DPS budget set by its level requirement, so upgrades genuinely upgrade: AoE weapons spread the budget across their targets (cone cleavers hit up to 4, the gravity maul slams all 5 around you, pellets/pierce/chain count the same way) and crit damage now scales with cadence — quick blades crit light (x1.6) while slow heavy hitters crit like trucks (x2.5).
