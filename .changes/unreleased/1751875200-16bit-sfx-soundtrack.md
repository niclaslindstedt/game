type: Changed
title: 16-bit soundtrack and SFX
---

The soundtrack and sound effects moved from an 8-bit NES palette to a 16-bit SNES-style sound: both themes are rearranged as ~2-minute multi-section scores (verse/chorus/breakdown arrangements in per-track score files) played by an upgraded sequencer with instrument patches, detuned chorus voices, vibrato, stereo pan, filtered drum kits, and a shared echo bus, and every sound effect gained layered 16-bit detail.
