---
type: Changed
title: Powerups, medkits, and dings scale with the campaign
---

Powerup damage now grows with your level exactly as the horde's healthbars do (and INTELLIGENCE deepens it, widening the stasis field too), medkits come in D2-style tiers — light, standard, large, superior — with bigger kits dropping off deeper content, and level-ups grant more stat points the higher you climb (one extra per ten levels).
