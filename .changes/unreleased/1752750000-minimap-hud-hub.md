type: Changed
title: Live minimap HUD hub
---

The upper-right map icon is now a live World-of-Warcraft-style minimap: the actual fog-of-war level in a rounded frame, still tappable to open the full map. The run's edge widgets hang off its border — the survival timer at the top (which also pauses the run), the kill count as a bare number at the bottom, and RAMPAGE as a gauge that fills and reddens around the frame instead of the old row of pips.
