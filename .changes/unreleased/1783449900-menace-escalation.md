title: Menace — the horde escalates when you overpower it
---

Overkilling monsters (dumping far more damage than their remaining hp into the killing blow) and keeping up a fast kill pace now bank **menace**, a meter that answers a runaway-strong player and bleeds off when you ease up. As it climbs it **lures** a denser, bigger crowd onto you (and every overkill drags nearby foes in at once), **evolves** freshly-spawned minions into tougher versions worth more XP and better loot, and — folded in with your own level — scales elite mobs and bosses to your power when they engage, so the set-piece fights stop melting. A RAMPAGE gauge on the HUD, a pulsing aura on evolved mobs, and an escalation cue mark the heat rising.
