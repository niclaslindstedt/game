---
type: Changed
title: Gentler speed scaling and a true-to-hit AoE cone
---

The weapon SPEED stat (DEX for melee/ranged, INT for magic) now quickens attacks about half as fast (+2% cadence per point instead of +4%), so pumping it sweetens fire rate rather than dominating a build. Melee swings now draw the exact area they strike — a filled cone from the player out to the weapon's true reach spanning its full arc — so the slash effect matches the hit geometry instead of a thin rim line.
