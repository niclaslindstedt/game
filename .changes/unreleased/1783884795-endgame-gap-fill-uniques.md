Twelve new world-drop uniques (ilvl 79–99) fill the 60→99 chase window where the roster ran thin: four charms and three bags — the slots that had no relic in the endgame before the artifacts — plus a head, two chest, a legs, and a feet piece. All drop on the JESUS rungs, farmed in the same rift → the bunker loop, roughly tripling the plain-unique variety at the level cap without changing the calibrated ~1-unique-per-run rate.
