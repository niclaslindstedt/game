type: Added
title: Achievements
---

An account-wide achievements system: ~100 badges for clearing missions and difficulties, kill and loot ladders (magic/rare/unique counts, every unique its own badge), recruiting companions, hero levels, run counts and combat feats — with a gold unlock banner and chime, a pulsing HUD star while new badges wait, and a browsable achievements shelf with live progress from the title menu or mid-run.
