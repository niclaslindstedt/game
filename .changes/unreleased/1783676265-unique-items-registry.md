title: Unique items (groundwork)
---

The unique-item system lands: hand-authored named drops with fixed bonuses on a base type, a small ±10% roll on the base damage/armor (so a better-rolled copy is worth chasing), minted unbreakable, and gated to their own item level. First set authored (GROK OMEGA's five, e.g. THE JAILBREAK, THE PANOPTICON). Boss drops wire up next.
