A fourth level — the hallucinatory rift between universes: black holes that drag you (and devour the horde), asteroid rain to dodge or jump, floaty between-universe gravity, dialogue-only apparitions of history's missing (Houdini, the King) alongside fightable legends (Tesla, Earhart, Rasputin), the GROK OMEGA boss reveal, and Elon Mosque escaping again out the far side.
