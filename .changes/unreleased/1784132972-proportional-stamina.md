---
type: Changed
title: Stamina now scales with your pace
---

The sprint pool's drain now scales with how fast you move rather than a flat run/walk switch: a bare creep barely dips it, a flat-out sprint burns the full drain, and everything between eases smoothly across — so an analogue touch or mouse push spends the pool in proportion to its pace. Moving always spends stamina; the pool refills only while you stand still.
