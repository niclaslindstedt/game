type: Changed
title: Mercy drops on easy and medium
---

Easy and medium now throw a struggling player a rope: a packed screen (20+ mobs) starts dropping screen-nuke bombs — up to 5% per kill on easy, 3% on medium — and as your health drains or your weapon nears breaking, medkits, plated armor, and repair kits rain harder. Hard and up get none of this help, so the fight stays as lethal as ever.
