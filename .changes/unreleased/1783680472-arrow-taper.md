---
type: Changed
title: Golden arrows taper and thin out on harder difficulties
---

Golden XP arrows now pay a shrinking share of the level bar as the hero climbs (a full quarter-level early, a thin sliver near the cap) and drop more seldom up the difficulty ladder — never on JESUS — so free levels fuel the onboarding without racing the whole campaign; the leveling-curve calculator now folds the arrow faucet into its pacing model.
