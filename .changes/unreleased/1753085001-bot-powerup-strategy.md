---
type: Changed
title: Autopilot plays powerups by value
---

The autopilot now ranks the powerup catalog (nuke > storm > orbit > stasis > magnet) and plays each pickup for what it's worth: the nuke waits for a real crowd, combat powers fire on a decent fight, the cheap stasis/magnet are spent eagerly, and low-value pickups are burned to keep a dock slot cycling free for stronger finds — the nuke is never wasted on shelf space. The merchant routine buys powerups in the same value order.
