---
type: Changed
title: Make quality is now a value range
---

Every plain weapon/armor drop now rolls a specific base-value multiplier inside its make quality's band (BROKEN → CRUDE → NORMAL → SUPERIOR → PERFECT), so two SUPERIOR copies of a base swing differently and the bands overlap between neighbours — a good CRUDE can out-swing a poor NORMAL. Unique and legendary items keep their static, hand-authored properties (no rolled affixes or suffixes); only their base damage/armor varies by the standing ±band.
