---
type: Changed
title: Difficulty balance
---

Rebalanced the difficulty ladder so every rung levels to its intended finish: leveling costs grow more gently at high level, each difficulty's XP bonus was retuned (nightmare no longer stalls ten levels under its maps — its late-campaign walls and unreachable bosses are gone), the moon/mars mob bands were lifted out of grey-XP territory, and nightmare EASTWORLD's band was eased so a normally-paced hero isn't fighting mobs ten levels over his head.
