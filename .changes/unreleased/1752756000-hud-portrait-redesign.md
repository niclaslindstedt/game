---
type: Changed
title: WoW-style portrait HUD
---

The upper-left HUD was rebuilt as a compact WoW-style unit: a framed hero-bust portrait (torso and above) with the level tucked on its lower-left corner, the health (red), mana (blue), and stamina (white) bars merged into one color-coded plate beside it, a themed sci-fi frame behind the portrait and bars, the held weapon floating below the portrait with its durability shown as a ring around the icon, and the bag moved onto the minimap corner as a pouch showing the free-slot count. The XP bar now hugs the very top edge, and the vitals animate smoothly.
