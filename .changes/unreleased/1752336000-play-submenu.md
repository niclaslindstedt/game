---
type: Changed
title: PLAY submenu — NEW GAME / LOAD GAME
---

The title menu's PLAY entry now opens a submenu: **NEW GAME** mints a fresh hero, **LOAD GAME** picks (or retires) a saved one. CONTINUE was renamed **RESUME** (shown only when a run is parked), and the standalone CHARACTERS entry is gone — the two PLAY paths make that choice explicit. Achievements can no longer be opened from the in-run HUD (the gold star and its Y shortcut were removed); browse them from the main menu's ACHIEVEMENTS shelf, which is where the account-wide badges belong.
