---
type: Changed
title: Queued pickup cards
---

Bag-gear pickup cards now queue and show one at a time instead of the newest replacing whatever was on screen, so a burst of loot no longer flash-hides earlier finds — each piece gets its own turn. Ordinary cards show for half as long while a backlog waits behind them so the queue drains quickly, while a better find (an upgrade at or above the worn piece for its slot) lingers longer for a proper look and a chance to tap-to-equip.
