title: Moon reforged into ridges and basins
---

THE MOON is rebuilt around discovery: three long rock ridges with offset pass-gaps carve the open regolith into basins the hero weaves through on an authored path from the lander to ARMSTRONG, the finite ridge-gap spawners ramp wisps → ghosts → wraiths → OPTIMUSK, and two off-path detour pockets (the CRASHED LANDER and THE THIRTEENTH GRAVE) each hide a chest guarded by a pinned rare/unique, with a STILL POINT merchant nook before the boss.
