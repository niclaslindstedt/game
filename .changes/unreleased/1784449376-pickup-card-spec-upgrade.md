---
type: Changed
title: Spec-aware pickup upgrades
---

The pickup card's UPGRADE marker (and the inventory upgrade glow) now weighs a
find's +STAT rolls by the stats your build has actually invested in, so an
off-spec piece no longer flashes UPGRADE — a caster's +INTELLECT find reads as
the upgrade a +STRENGTH one doesn't. A find that's clearly worse for your slot
also drops its "TAP TO EQUIP" prompt, and the UPGRADE tag's ▲ arrow renders
correctly instead of a "?" (a missing font glyph).
