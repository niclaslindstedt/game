---
type: Changed
title: The difficulty ladder turns every knob
---

Difficulties now differ across the whole run, not just monster counts: a stat head-start on EASY, flatter mob counts with a RELATIVE monster level per rung (EASY fields mobs three levels under you, JESUS two above — the gap never closes), thinner medkit/armor/powerup drops and richer rare/epic/legendary rolls up the ladder, a faster stamina burn, fading dodge/hit rates, and a rampage meter that triggers easier, cools slower, and hits harder on the top rungs.
