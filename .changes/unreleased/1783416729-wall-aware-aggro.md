---
type: Changed
title: Walls hide you, ghosts don't care
---

Monsters no longer aggro through walls the player can't jump over — waking on proximity needs line of sight, ghostly moon mobs excepted: they sense (and drift) straight through stone, including the new boulder ridge walls raised across the moonscape. Packs also squeeze 20% tighter, so a kited horde bunches into one clump.
