---
type: Changed
title: Item cards reworked, unique finds shine
---

Unique and legendary items now glow in the inventory, shop, and arsenal grids and on their item cards, and every magic-or-better card names its quality tier at the foot. Item cards also got tighter: the weapon-class line is now a glyph beside the name (sword/reticle/spark), the item level moved to the lower right, and the REQUIRES LEVEL line color-codes how current the piece is (red above you, yellow current, green recent, grey outgrown).
