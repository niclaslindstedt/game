---
type: Changed
title: Strict stamina paces
---

Stamina is now a strict three-pace ladder: any RUNNING pace burns the full drain rate, a WALK (half speed) regains only a 0.1x trickle, and STANDING still refills at the full rate — and an emptied pool now demands 2 seconds of uninterrupted standstill before regen resumes (any movement restarts the wait), so managing the sprint pool really matters.
