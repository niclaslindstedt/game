---
type: Added
title: Scenario art staging
---

Test scenarios (`?scenario=`) can now stage art judgements, not just bug repros: `freeze` poses the world's actors for a stable screenshot (the hero stays playable), `spawns[].hpFrac` places pre-wounded mobs to show battle-damage stages, `drops` lays ground pickups (loose kinds, equipment at a chosen tier, named uniques, powerups, story items) around the hero, and `place: "merchant"` teleports to the trader's stall.
