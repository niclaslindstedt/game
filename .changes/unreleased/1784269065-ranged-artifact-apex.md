type: Changed
title: FAIL-NOT is a real ranged apex
---

The rarest ranged relic, FAIL-NOT, spent its whole power budget on utility (never-miss, a spark proc, dexterity) with no raw damage — so the top of the ranged artifact ladder hit softest of the three. It now carries a heavy damage bonus, making it the marksman's boss-killer apex (the ranged answer to DURENDAL) so a ranged endgame has a weapon worth chasing.
