---
type: Changed
title: AIM & SHOOT mouse mode
---

The hold-to-steer mouse mode is now AIM & SHOOT — WASD walks, the pointer aims, and the left button is the trigger, with a new desktop-only AUTO-FIRE toggle (on by default) that keeps the character firing on its own; the mouse-scheme settings rows (MOUSE, AUTO-FIRE, KEYS) are now hidden on touch devices like KEY BINDINGS already was.
