---
type: Changed
title: Elites and bosses give a real chunk of a level
---

Elite and boss kills now pay XP as a SHARE OF THE HERO'S CURRENT LEVEL BAR (elites ~12%, bosses ~20%) instead of a small hp-proportional lump, so a set-piece kill visibly lurches the XP bar the same noticeable amount on every map and difficulty — and because the reward reads the live level, it can't go stale as the hero out-levels a map, while the per-map XP cap still fades it to nothing on outgrown replays so boss farming never over-levels.
