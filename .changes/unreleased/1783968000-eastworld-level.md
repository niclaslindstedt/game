title: "Level 5: EASTWORLD"
---

The campaign finale — the rift's far side is EASTWORLD, a knockoff wild-west theme park built by Vladimir Putain and Steven Seagull and run on ZAI robotics: a tight frontier town of building-sized houses, robot cowboy hosts, celebrity elites (Seagull, Putain and his sellable brand watches, Gerald Depardieu), Elon Mosque finally dying (dropping the new zero-stat TRASH tier), a merchant stall that fences Putain's estate at unique odds, and THE ZAI SUPERCORE boss — shielded by three cover-taking GROK controllers that shoot back — whose death quakes the park and plays the campaign's black-screen epilogue.
