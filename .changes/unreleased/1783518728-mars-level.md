title: "Level 3: MARS"
---

The campaign continues to Mars: a secret SpaceZ colony where rovers work the red dust outside and robots and fembots staff the base inside — three tech-billionaire elites (LARRY WEBPAGE, BUILD GATES, PETER SEAL), a locked lizard shrine, a new desert-western theme ("RED DUST"), and ELON MOSQUE, the first boss who doesn't die: he cowers, reveals what Ada was traded for, and flees through a rift. ARMSTRONG's moon ending now points the way (the moon was SpaceZ's disastrous mistake), and the OPTIMUS robots are now called OPTIMUSK.
