---
type: Changed
title: Tame the magic mid/late DPS runaway
---

Magic weapons now quicken with INTELLIGENCE at a discounted rate, so a deep-INT caster no longer out-scales the melee and ranged lanes by ~5× in the late game — INT was double-dipping (scaling damage AND cadence on the same points). Magic still leads its stretch of the game, just no longer by a blowout.
