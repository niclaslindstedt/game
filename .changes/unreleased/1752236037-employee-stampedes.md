type: Added
title: Employee stampedes on SpaceZ HQ
---

SpaceZ HQ now has an "asteroid"-style hazard: herds of five panicked employees (three sprite variants) charge across the aisles right-to-left at great speed with a dust cloud boiling off their back, trampling and killing any minions in their lane and knocking any elite/boss aside. A grounded hero caught by a herd loses ~20% of his max health and is knocked down for two seconds — jump over the wall or step out of its lane to avoid it. The autopilot hops incoming herds on its own.
