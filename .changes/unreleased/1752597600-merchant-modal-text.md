title: Larger, more legible merchant shop
---

The merchant shop is redesigned for phones: the text is bigger and easier to read (stall prices, purse, and the merchant name and section headers all read at a comfortable size), coin prices are vertically centered against their coin icon, SELL JUNK and SELL ALL move to a bottom action row beside CLOSE, and REPAIR becomes a wrench slot among the merchant's goods with its price underneath.
