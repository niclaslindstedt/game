---
type: Fixed
title: Broken weapons no longer desync your health and stamina bars
---

A weapon that granted bonus STAMINA (and the health/sprint pools it deepens) left those pools sized to it even after it broke out of your hand, so a drink or heal could top off to a stale maximum until the next level-up or stat spend re-derived them. Breaking a weapon now re-derives the pools immediately, exactly like equipping or unequipping one.
