---
type: Added
title: Seasoned arrival on later levels
---

Starting a level past the campaign opener now spawns the hero as if he really cleared the levels before it: a player level derived from their rosters (mob counts × hp through the XP curve), stat points auto-spent, and the previous level's kit in hand — its signature weapon, its suit and charm, and a couple of its powerups. Arriving on Mars means arriving with the MOON'S BLADE.
