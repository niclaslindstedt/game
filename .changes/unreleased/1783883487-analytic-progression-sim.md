type: Added
title: Analytic progression simulator
---

A paper-playthrough balance tool (`scripts/progression-sim.mjs`, engine side `src/sim/analytic.ts`) that uses the real kill funnel to farm every mob a level fields — the horde, its elites, rolled rare/unique visitors, and its boss — across the whole campaign to level 99, snapshotting the hero's full stat block every N kills and rendering a self-contained HTML progression graph.
