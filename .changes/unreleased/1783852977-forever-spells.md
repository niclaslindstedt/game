type: Added
title: Forever spells, procs & sure strike
---

Unique and legendary items can now grant permanent powers: forever spells (circling flame, stormcall, stasis field) that run while the piece is worn — scaling with rank and firing faster and harder with INTELLIGENCE — plus on-hit/on-kill lightning and nova procs and SURE STRIKE (the weapon never whiffs). Level-99+ legendaries mint bigger off deeper kills, and a legendary's drop odds now follow its power: the stronger the item, the rarer the find.
