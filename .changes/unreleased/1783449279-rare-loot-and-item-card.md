---
type: Added
title: Rare (yellow) loot, item discarding, and a richer item card
---

Yellow **RARE** weapons and gear now drop — a tier above magic that rolls two affixes (including stat and life rolls on weapons), turning up on the moon and from elites and bosses, never the level-1 rank and file. Epic and legendary rolls widen to three and four affixes to match. Magic+ items are named Diablo-style from their affixes ("CRUEL PIPE OF THE FOX") instead of a flat "MAGIC" prefix, and the inventory item card now shows the item's icon, a rarity-colored name, per-attribute colors on each magic property, and attack speed as plain seconds between hits. Drag a bag item out of the panel and drop it on the ground to destroy it for good.
