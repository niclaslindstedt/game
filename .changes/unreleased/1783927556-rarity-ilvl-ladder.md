---
type: Changed
title: D2-style rarity item-level ladder
---

Item power now sits a rarity-scaled margin above the level where it drops, D2-style: rolled MAGIC finds roll a hair over the loot level (+0-2) and RARE a clear step over (+3-5); every bottom-tier boss and world UNIQUE is re-pitched to about ten levels over where you now reach its level — so early drops land as level-appropriate upgrades you grow into, instead of dropping too weak or barely at all — and the named LEGENDARIES are normalized to about twenty levels over their equip requirement, keeping their signature procs and spells. SENTINEL'S GREAVES, which outgrew the bottom tier, moves to the nightmare rung.
