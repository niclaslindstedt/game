---
type: Changed
title: D2-style rarity item-level ladder
---

Item power now sits a rarity-scaled margin above the level where it drops, D2-style: rolled MAGIC finds roll a hair over the loot level (+0-2) and RARE a clear step over (+3-5), while every bottom-tier boss UNIQUE is re-pitched to about ten levels over where you now reach its boss — so early-boss drops land as level-appropriate upgrades you grow into, instead of dropping too weak or barely at all. SENTINEL'S GREAVES, which outgrew the bottom tier, moves to the nightmare rung.
