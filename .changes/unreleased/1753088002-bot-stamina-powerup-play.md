---
type: Changed
title: Autopilot paces itself and curates its powerup dock
---

The autopilot now walks to recover stamina on quiet ground and only opens up to a run once the pool is rested (~70%+); it saves the nuke for a genuinely overwhelming flood (~20+ mobs inside the blast), fires stasis when cornered (winded, under half health, a pack hunting), drops a lesser powerup to make room when a better one lies in reach (a running slot is freed for free), and keeps its dock sorted in its own priority order so you can see how it ranks what it carries.
