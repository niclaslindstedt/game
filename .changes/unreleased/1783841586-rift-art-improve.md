title: The Rift's aviator, mad monk, and star jelly redrawn

Redrew three of THE RIFT's weakest-reading sprites: AMELIA EARHART now reads as a WWII aviator (leather flight cap, brass goggle lenses, sheepskin collar, streaming scarf) instead of a generic figure, GRIGORI RASPUTIN looms as a heavy hooded mad-monk with burning eyes, a grey beard, and a gold Orthodox cross that separates from the void ground, and the STAR JELLY gains a glowing star core and flowing gradient tentacles.
