title: Autopilot pocket arsenal
---

The autoplay bot now banks its best ranged and one magic weapon in the bag and swaps its held weapon to whatever maximizes damage each moment — the blade when a body stands in blade reach, the pocket shot while closing in, kiting, fetching loot, or airborne mid-jump (where a melee blade can't swing at all), with a single-target round preferred when a boss is near — never touching worn gear, carrying the attack cooldown across swaps so juggling mints no free shots, and keeping the bag sorted like the powerup dock: pockets in the first two slots, then the loot ordered by preciousness.
