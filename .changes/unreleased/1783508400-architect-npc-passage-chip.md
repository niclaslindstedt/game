title: THE ARCHITECT and the PASSAGE CHIP
---

A fifth elite joins SpaceZ HQ: THE ARCHITECT, the hero's old bench partner brainwashed into building the company's superintelligence. He rushes into view for a confrontation — the hero begs him to quit the evil company, he answers that humans are obsolete and "now you will die" — then fights and drops the PASSAGE CHIP he cut into his own skull, a new passive trinket that grants +1 INTELLIGENCE while it merely rides in the bag.
