---
type: Changed
title: Golden arrows are a rarer catch-up drop
---

Golden XP arrows now drop about once every fifty kills (down from ~one in twenty-four) and act as a catch-up faucet: they pay a share of your next level only while you're under-levelled for the map, then go cold — a handful of kills' worth — once you reach the level a normal run of that map and difficulty leaves you at, so grinding old ground can no longer over-level you.
