type: Changed
title: BOT VIEW shows one steady thought
---

The developer BOT VIEW overlay now resolves the autopilot's raw per-tick decision into a single overarching thought instead of strobing between neighbouring branches: a hero dancing at a pack's edge reads as one "SKIRMISH" rather than flickering "KITE" / "GIVE GROUND", a hazard dodge or emergency bail preempts and is held briefly so it stays legible, and otherwise the most-sustained recent intent wins.
