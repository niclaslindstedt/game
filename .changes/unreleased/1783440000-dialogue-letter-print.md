type: Added
title: Letter-by-letter dialogue and briefings
---

Dialogue boxes and level-intro briefings now print one character at a time with a quiet 16-bit letter-print blip, holding a dramatic beat on ellipses, full stops, and dashes so a briefing or a dying elite's last words land with the timing they were written for; a tap finishes the crawl (in dialogue the next tap then turns the page), and reduced-motion readers get the whole page at once.
