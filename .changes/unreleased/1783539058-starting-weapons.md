type: Added
title: The wall arsenal — a starting weapon per difficulty
---

The weapon on the hero's living-room wall now depends on the chosen difficulty — HAIRY POTTER'S WAND (easy), MEDIEVAL SWORD (medium), COMBAT KNIFE (hard), BRASS KNUCKLES (nightmare), A STICK (JESUS CHRIST!) — and the prelude cutscene always shows the exact piece the run starts with.
