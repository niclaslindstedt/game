---
type: Changed
title: Slower base fire rate and a denser opening
---

Ranged and magic weapons — the starting blaster most of all — now fire noticeably slower out of the box, and SpaceZ HQ opens with a thick crowd of staff packed around the spawn instead of a sparse trickle, so standing still no longer clears the horde for free. A build wins the fire rate back by investing in attack speed (see the stat overhaul in this release). On MEDIUM a stationary player is overrun within about 20 seconds on both levels, and sooner on higher difficulties.
