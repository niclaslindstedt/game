---
type: Changed
title: Slower base fire rate, scaled by DEXTERITY/INTELLIGENCE
---

Ranged and magic weapons — the starting blaster most of all — now fire noticeably slower out of the box, so standing still no longer clears the horde for free. DEXTERITY (ranged), INTELLECT (magic), and STRENGTH (melee) now quicken their class's attack cadence as well as its damage, so a build wins the fire rate back. On MEDIUM a stationary player is overrun within about 20 seconds, and sooner on higher difficulties.
