---
type: Changed
title: STRENGTH hits harder, but weighs you down
---

**STRENGTH** now raises physical (melee & ranged) damage more steeply than INTELLIGENCE raises magic damage — raw firepower is STR's one payoff, so a bruiser out-hits a mage per point. In exchange, every point of muscle **slows your walk** a little (floored so a pure-STR build still moves), so STRENGTH and SPEED now pull against each other instead of stacking for free.
