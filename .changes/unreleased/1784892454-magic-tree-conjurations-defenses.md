title: The Archon's full arsenal
---

The INTELLIGENCE tree fills out into a hands-free killing engine: SEEKER ORBS hunt the horde and burst on impact, an IMMOLATION AURA scorches everything that closes on you, and an ARCANE SINGULARITY drags the swarm into a crushing vortex — all always on and scaling with rank and INTELLIGENCE. Two defensive talents close the tree: FROST NOVA freezes the foes around you solid the instant you're struck, and ARCANE RETRIBUTION turns a growing share of every blow back on its owner. A deep-INT hero can now stand in the middle of the horde and let the spells do all the work.
