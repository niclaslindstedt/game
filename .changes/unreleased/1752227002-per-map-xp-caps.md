---
type: Added
title: Per-map XP level caps
---

Every level × difficulty pair now has a hero-level ceiling: XP earned on that map halves per level across the approach to the cap and stops entirely at it, so re-running an outgrown easy map still rains loot but never levels you past its cap. Caps are sized above where a first campaign pass naturally lands (easy tops at 10–22 across its maps, JESUS runs to the global cap), so the story never starves — only the rerun grind hits the wall.
