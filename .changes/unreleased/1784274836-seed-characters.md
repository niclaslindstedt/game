The hidden DEVELOPER menu gains a SEED CHARACTERS page that mints ready-to-play melee, ranged, and magic heroes at the NIGHTMARE (LV 34), JESUS (LV 56), POST-JESUS (LV 70), and ENDGAME (LV 99) tiers, each with a lane-optimized stat spread and level-appropriate gear.
