Retire the tap-to-cast spell system and the mana pool it ran on — the hero now
fights with his weapon and always-on passive talents. The HUD spell bar, the
mana bar, mana potions (the blue gatorade), and their low-mana mercy drops are
gone; SPIRIT keeps driving out-of-combat health regen. Adopted characters keep
their stats and convert their old spell investment into talent points.
