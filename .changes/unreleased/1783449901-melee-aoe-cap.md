---
type: Changed
title: Melee cleave now hits two foes until INT widens it
---

A melee swing no longer strikes every monster in its cone — it lands on the two nearest by default, and each point of INTELLIGENCE raises that cap by one. Widening the cone still lets a swing _reach_ more of the crowd; INT is now what lets it actually _cleave_ them, so a wide STRENGTH sword stops clearing the whole horde for free.
