type: Added
title: Fog of war
---

The level now has Warcraft-style fog of war: ground you've uncovered stays fully clear, ground you've never seen is solid black, and the frontier between them is a soft dithered transition band — and mobs standing out in that band (or the dark beyond it) stay hidden until they step onto ground you can see. The map uncovers as a circle sweeping the path you walk.
