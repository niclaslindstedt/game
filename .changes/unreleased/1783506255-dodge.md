---
type: Added
title: Dodge — sidestep a blow entirely
---

Heroes can now DODGE an enemy's hit outright, taking no damage or armor loss at all. Every hero starts with a small innate chance; **DEXTERITY** sharpens it and **LUCK** nudges it up marginally. A dodged blow floats a "DODGE" tag with a clean whiff, and the inventory stat panel shows your current dodge chance.
