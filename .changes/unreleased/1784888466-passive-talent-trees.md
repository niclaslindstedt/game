---
type: Added
title: Passive talent trees
---

Every 10 points a hero puts into STRENGTH, DEXTERITY, or INTELLIGENCE now earns a talent point in that stat's tree, spent through a full-tree picker at level-up on always-on passive talents (up to rank 5 each) — a WoW-style Warlord / Windrunner / Archon spread that grows alongside a build: melee crit and enrage, ranged crit, dodge and move speed, flat damage reduction, a deeper health pool, and a magic ward. Spent points are permanent and lock a floor under their earning stat during a respec; adopted veterans convert their existing stat investment into a pile of picks on load.
