---
type: Changed
title: Autopilot treats stamina as fight fuel
---

The autopilot now sprints freely in the open while keeping a tunable ~20% reserve (walking it back at the floor), and tops up before fights instead of pacing everywhere: a pack spotted ahead is engaged at a full pool — it walks the approach when the walk regen still refills before contact, or plants a breather and lets the mobs cover the ground when it can't.
