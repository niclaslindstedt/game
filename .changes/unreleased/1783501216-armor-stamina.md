---
type: Added
title: Armor and stamina
---

Suits now grant an armor pool (green/yellow/red grades) that soaks a share of every physical hit before it reaches HP, shown as its own HUD bar; a new stamina pool lets you run at full speed until it drains and refills while you ease off, and the level-up HEALTH stat is now STAMINA that deepens the pool and quickens its recovery.
