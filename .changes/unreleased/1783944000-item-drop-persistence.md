---
type: Added
title: Kept items survive balance changes
---

Item drops you keep now stay exactly as they dropped, even after a later build rebalances or removes their base — a found weapon or gear is never nerfed or lost out from under you, and only NEW drops feel a loot change. Each drop is minted with a frozen snapshot of its stats, so kept inventory, banked loadouts, keepsakes, and a resumed run all carry through catalog edits unchanged.
