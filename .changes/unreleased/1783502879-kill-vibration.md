type: Added
title: Haptic feedback on kills and dialogue
---

Phones with a vibration motor now buzz on kills — scaled by the mob's rarity, from a minion's flick to a boss's rolling rumble — and tick under each letter of the dialogue typewriter crawl so a line is felt as it types. Toggle it under SETTINGS → CONTROLS; it is silently inert on iOS, which has no web Vibration API.
