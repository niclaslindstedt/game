---
type: Changed
title: Hardcore campaign high scores
---

High scores are now hardcore-only and span a whole campaign — foes felled, survival time and peak menace summed across every map of a difficulty and banked when the campaign is beaten (SURVIVED) or the hero falls (FELL); the survival clock only ticks while a fight is live (a foe on the field, or within two seconds of the last kill), so a cleared field can't be milked for time.
