---
type: Fixed
title: Companions no longer trigger RAMPAGE
---

A companion's damage and kills are now kept out of the menace meter — the RAMPAGE escalation answers an overpowered hero, so a party carrying the fight (like a powerup's screen-nuke) no longer heats the meter, jolts the overkill lure, or advances the evolution ratchet. Their kills still book into the run stats and pay the hero XP as before.
