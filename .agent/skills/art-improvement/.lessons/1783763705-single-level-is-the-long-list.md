---
title: A single level can BE the whole 30-sprite long list
date: 2026-07-11
---

Some levels have ~30 total sprites, so the survey sheet already *is* the
"worst 30" and the 30 → 20 → 10 funnel collapses. When the genuinely weak
count is under 10, do NOT pad the finalists to hit 10 — the "don't flatten
hierarchy" craft note applies. Name the real worst (often 5–7) and leave
sprites that already read well alone.
