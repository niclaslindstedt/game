---
title: Wounds are auto-generated — draw the base to survive them
date: 2026-07-11
---

`variants <name>` shows the `_hurt`/`_wrecked`/`_dying` stages; the
generator paints them from the base per the enemy's `gore` field (staff
bleed red `r/i`, machines throw gold sparks `y/Y`, the haunting smears
cyan). Never hand-draw a wound stage — confirm the *base* silhouette still
reads once the overlay lands, and animated mobs need BOTH frames (`_0` and
`_1`) redrawn to match.
