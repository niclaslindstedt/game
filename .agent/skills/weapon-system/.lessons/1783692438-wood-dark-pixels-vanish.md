---
title: Wood-dark pixels vanish on weapon icons
date: 2026-07-10
---

The core `k` wood char is near-outline dark; weapon hafts/stocks read better
in the warm `B` brown. Verify every icon at @8x — first drafts of "obvious"
silhouettes (rayguns, revolvers) read as crosses and blobs; two or three
iterations is normal.
