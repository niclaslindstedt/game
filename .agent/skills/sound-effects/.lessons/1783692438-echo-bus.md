---
title: The echo bus is the biggest "sounds 16-bit now" knob — keep combat sends low
date: 2026-07-10
---

One shared feedback delay (0.22 s, feedback 0.32, lowpass 2600) in
`synth.ts`; `echo:` on any tone/noise is a send level into it. It is the
single biggest "sounds 16-bit now" knob — and the easiest to overdose: keep
combat SFX ≤0.3.
