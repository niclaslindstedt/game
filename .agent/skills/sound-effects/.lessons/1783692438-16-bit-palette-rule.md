---
title: The 16-bit palette rule — waveform roles
date: 2026-07-10
---

The palette opened up from the old NES three-waves rule — sines are the
gloss/bell layer, saws carry danger and weight (playerHurt, kills, boss),
squares stay the action core, triangle stays the warm reward/bass voice.
What keeps it "chip" is short envelopes and quantized pitches, not banning
waveforms.
